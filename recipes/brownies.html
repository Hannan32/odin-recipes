Brownies

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of all-purpose flour.
  • Â¾ cups of good-quality sweetened cocoa powder.
  • Baking powder acts as a leavener, which means it helps the vegan brownies rise.
  • Salt enhances the overall flavor of the brownies, but it will not make them taste salty.
  • a cup of hot water (or a cup of brewed coffee for more flavor).
  • Vegetable oils keeps these vegan brownies nice and moist
  • Vanilla extract takes the flavor up a notch.

Steps

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and stir until the batter is blended.
  3. Pour the batter into a prepared baking dish and spread evenly.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, 15 to 20 minutes.
    Cool in the pan set over a wire rack.
  5. Let the brownies cool before cutting them into squares.
